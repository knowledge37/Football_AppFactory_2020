KAIZER CHIEFS FC (AMAKHOSI)

Kaizer Chiefs Football Club is a South African professional football club based in Naturena
in Johannesburg that plays in the Premier Soccer League.
The team is nicknamed Amakhosi which means "Lords" or "Chiefs" in Zulu,
and the Phefeni Glamour Boys. Chiefs have won 4 league titles and over 50 club trophies
